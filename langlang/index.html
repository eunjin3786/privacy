Privacy Policy

LangLang

LangLang respects your privacy. Your personal data stays on your device.

Data Collection

LangLang is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not directly collect any personal data from our users.

  • No personal information is collected by us
  • No usage data is tracked by us
  • No analytics services are used

Local Data Storage

All your data stays on your device:

  • Translations: Processed locally using Apple's Translation framework
  • Example Sentences: Generated on-device using Apple Intelligence
  • Pinned Items: Stored locally on your device only
  • Settings: Saved locally on your device

Advertising

LangLang displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information to serve ads:

  • Device Identifier: Used for ad delivery
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about ads viewed

This data is not linked to your identity and is not used for tracking purposes.

For more information, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

LangLang uses Google AdMob for advertising. Apart from this, no other third-party services collect user data.
